About

I am an experimental physicist with expertise in molecular beam epitaxy (MBE), nanofabrication, and advanced magnetotransport measurements. My research focuses on topological phenomena in kagome lattice compounds and their potential applications in quantum technologies.

My current work involves investigating nonlinear Hall responses, anomalous in-plane Hall effects, and Berry curvature phenomena in magnetic Weyl semimetals and altermagnets at room temperature.
